Pet Age Calculator

We all know pets age differently than us. But how differently does time flow in their perception? With this pet age calculator you can find out just how old your goodest boy/girl is! It uses formulas different for cats and dogs specifically to give you the answer you were looking for.
In addition, the friendly design with carefully illustrated icons gives the website a playful and welcoming feeling.

Simple Card Game

Card game created for indecisive people faced with tough choices. The game features 2 players pulling a card from an entire deck of cards using random numbers. The dynamic title offers the conclusion quickly, in order to speed things up. Did you know the King card is 'stronger' than the Queen? Even the cards without numbers on them still have an order!

Notes and Quotes

Notes and Quotes is a simple note taking app for both creative and forgetful people. It has a simple design and layout in order not to let users forget their ideas before writing them down.
The app is designed with React components and pieced together with Javascript and CSS.
